Preventative Maintenance

1. Check game manual for special maintenance needs
2. Go into game test menu and test all functions
3. Inspect all fans for cleanliness and operation including cabinet, power supply, and computer fans
4. Vacuum or blow out all dust in cabinet
5. Check operation and cleanliness of all controls including buttons, belts, joysticks, pedals, guns, etc.
6. Verify operation of all lights
7. Verify power supply voltage
8. Check for any loose or missing screws or other hardware
9. Clean all glass surfaces with glass cleaner
10. Clean all other surfaces with standard disinfectant
11. Vacuum under game
12. Test game again through test menu
13. Play game to verify playability
